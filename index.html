    • What is SEO?

    SEO = Search Engine Optimization


    Definition: "SEO is the process of affecting the visibility of a website or a web page in a search engine's unpaid results - often referred to as "natural," "organic," or "earned" results." - Wikipedia

    Process - On-going monitoring effort.

    Un-Paid - You can't pay Google to be #1.

    SEO vs. SEM

    SEM = Overall Marketing efforts on the search engines

    SEO = Optimization efforts to improve visibility
    Source: http://upcity.com/blog/seo-or-ppc/

    2003 - 2005 Unethical Optimization Tactics

    2010 - 2013 - Focus on user experience.

    SEO Terminology

    Keyword (or Search Query)
    A word or phrase that is entered into a search engine

    Organic Listing
    A result that shows on the search results page.

    Ranking
    The position of a listing within the search engine results


    Source: http://searchenginewatch.com/static/glossary#

    SEO Terminology

    Algorithm - A program that delivers results within a search engine

    Best Practices

  • DO use relevant anchor text
  • DO link to deeper pages
  • Every page should be accessible by at least one internal link on-site
  • DO NOT overdo it- link naturally
  • DO NOT stuff your footer with links

    Backlinks & Link Building

    Linking

    Be Careful!

    Remember the Penguin update?

  • Google has cracking down on spammy links. Make sure your link building efforts are for quality links
  • Try to create good content that entices linking and sharing to organically build a link profile
